Every Tenth Matters

Basic Information

Instrumentation: Choral (TBB)
Duration: 4:10
Year composed: 2024

Description

Written for the Brooklyn Youth Chorus Bass Ensemble, performed March 15, 2025

The 2023 Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change report was dire, but as researcher Wolfgang Cramer said after its release, “1.5C will always be better than 1.6C...Every tenth of a degree matters.” A stable climate can feel like an impossibility, but if we can imagine a better world, we can make it a reality, one tenth of a degree at a time. This piece affirms that our collective action truly does make a difference.

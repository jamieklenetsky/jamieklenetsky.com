Cape Spear

Basic Information

Instrumentation: Violin, viola, cello and bass
Duration: 7:50
Year composed: 2005

Description

A work for strings inspired by the awe-inspiring beauty of Cape Spear, the easternmost point of North America, upon a visit to Newfoundland. The piece draws from the Irish and unique Newfie heritage of Cape Spear, as well as its natural beauty.

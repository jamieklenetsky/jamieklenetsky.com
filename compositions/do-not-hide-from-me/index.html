Do Not Hide From Me

Basic Information

Instrumentation: Choral (SSAATTBB)
Duration: 2:30
Year composed: 2011

Description

A commission from the Seton Hall Chapel Choir, performed on June 4, 2011, St. Paul's Roman Catholic Church, Princeton, NJ

This is a setting of a classic Christian text, commissioned for performance at an evening mass.

Lyrics

Lord, hear my voice when I call to you.
My heart has prompted me to seek your face;
I seek it, Lord.
Do not hide from me. Alleluia.
Glory to the Father, and to the son. And to the Holy Spirit.
As it was in the beginning, is now and will be forever.
Amen.

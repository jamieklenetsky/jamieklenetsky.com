Voice

Jamie has sung with C4: The Choral Composer/Conductor Collective for 13 years, and primarily focuses on learning and performing new choral music.

Other choral work includes:

  • professional chamber choir, Vocala
  • early music ensemble, Polyhymnia.
  • artist-in-residence with the Continuo Arts Foundation (member of their "Sonare" ensemble)
  • professional chamber choir, Kinnara Ensemble

She has many years of church experience, having served as a section leader of the Westfield (NJ) Presbyterian Church choir and a member of the Seton Hall Chapel Choir. Jamie continues to sing at area churches when they’re in need of a strong alto!

She performed with Eastern Opera of New Jersey (now disbanded) for multiple productions, including Mamma Lucia in Cavalleria Rusticana and La suora zelatrice in Suor Angelica. 

She was a featured soloist of the Red Oak Contemporary Ensemble. Jamie has performed in new music workshops including the Breathmark Retreat, Creative Musicians Retreat, and Rhymes With Opera’s new music summer workshop.
